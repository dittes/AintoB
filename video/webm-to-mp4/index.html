About the Formats

🌐

WebM (WebM Video)

WebM is an open, royalty-free video format developed by Google for web delivery. It uses VP8/VP9 or AV1 video with Vorbis or Opus audio — providing excellent compression and quality for streaming without codec licensing costs.

Compatibility: Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Android. Limited or no support on Safari and iOS without workarounds.
