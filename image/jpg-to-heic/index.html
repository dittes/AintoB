About the Formats

🌅

JPG (Joint Photographic Experts Group)

JPG (or JPEG) is the world's most widely used image format for photographs. Lossy compression achieves small file sizes at the cost of some image detail — a trade-off that works well for natural scenes and photographs where minor quality loss is imperceptible.

Compatibility: Universal — supported by every browser, OS, device, and image application on the planet.

📷

HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container)

HEIC is the photo format used by Apple devices since iOS 11. Based on the HEIF standard with H.

Compatibility: Native on iOS 11+, macOS High Sierra+. Requires plugins or conversion for Windows and Android.
